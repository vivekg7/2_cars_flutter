## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

While using Our Service, We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to play the game.

#### Usage Data
We do not collect any Usage Data or analytics from your device. The game operates entirely offline.

#### Local Storage
The Application uses local device storage (SharedPreferences) solely to persist your high scores and game settings locally on your Device. This data is not transmitted to Us or any third party.

Since we do not collect Personal Data, we do not use it for any purpose.
